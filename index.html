About

Joy Long-Vidal, LPC, CTTS

Licensed Professional Counselor (9294) • Certified Tobacco Treatment Specialist (NT0568).

My approach to therapy focuses on providing a supportive and compassionate environment that fosters healing and growth.  I take a collaborative, strengths-based approach to helping my clients find their voice and regain a sense of control and pleasure in life. I believe that we are a team working together with one common goal - helping you feel better. Together, we help you develop tools and strategies for navigating emotions, relationships, and life goals to increase personal power and joy. My clinical style is a blend of Motivational Interviewing, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Solution Focused Therapy, Mind-Body Informed Framework, Mindfulness, and the use of Expressive Arts.

My experience includes working with children/adolescents, adults, and families in a variety of mental health capacities including community-based mental health, research, and medical settings.

Education & Training

Bachelors of Arts in Psychology, minor in Public Health from University of North Carolina-Greensboro
Masters of Arts in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from North Carolina Central University
Licensed Professional Counselor
Certified Tobacco Treatment Specialist
In-training as a Trauma Resiliency Model Practitioner : Level 2
Training in: Motivational Interviewing, Mindfulness Based Stress Reduction, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy
