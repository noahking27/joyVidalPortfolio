About

Joy Long-Vidal, LPC, TTS

Psychotherapy for Individuals, Couples, and Families

My approach to therapy focuses on providing a supportive and compassionate environment that fosters healing and growth. I take a collaborative, strengths-based approach to helping my clients find their power, build self-awareness, and develop wellness tools that to improve symptom management.

My clinical style is a blend of Motivational Interviewing, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Solution Focused Therapy, Mind-Body Informed Framework, Mindfulness and the use of Expressive Arts.

I am a Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) as well as a Tobacco Treatment Specialist (TTS). My experience includes working with adolescents, adults and families in a variety of mental health settings community-based mental health, research, and medical facilities.
