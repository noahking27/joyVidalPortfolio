About

Joy Long-Vidal, LPC, TTS

Certified Tobacco Treatment Specialist
Psychotherapy for Individuals, Couples, and Families

My approach to therapy focuses on providing a supportive and compassionate environment that fosters healing and growth. I take a collaborative, strengths-based approach to helping my clients find their voice and regain a sense of control and pleasure in life. I believe that we are a team working together with one common goal - helping you feel better. Together, we help you develop tools and strategies for navigating emotions, relationships, and life goals to increase personal power and joy.

My clinical style is a blend of Motivational Interviewing, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Solution Focused Therapy, Mind-Body Informed Framework, Mindfulness and the use of Expressive Arts.

I am a Licensed Professional Counselor (9294) as well as a Tobacco Treatment Specialist (NT0568). My experience includes working with children/adolescents, adults, and families in a variety of mental health settings community-based mental health, research, and medical facilities.
